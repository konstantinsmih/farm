Optimization Perks

Perk: (Category) Cost Notes
Forester III-IV, Orchard Land I 30, 50, 50 Orchard is one of the most important production buildings. Combined with Forester I & II, Forester III & IV can give a total +30% bonus to production.
Orchard Land II & III 125, 250 Good to buy as your inventory size outgrows your orchard size. See Orchard Land I above.
Grape Juice Pitcher 100 Grape Juice uses are important at all stages and stack additively. More uses mean more crops and faster progression!
Grape Juice Fountain & Waterfall 200, 300 Pricey additions; purchase depends on play style. Highly recommended if you find yourself waiting for long-growing crops.
Extra Wishes (I-II) 30, 125 Increases daily throws. Great for getting rare items like Runestones early. All Extra Wish perks stack.
Exploring Almanac 30 Exploring is notoriously slow to level. This perk is essential for hitting 4x99 faster, though it becomes redundant after level 99.
Sprint Shoes I 100 Improves efficiency and reduces manual tapping. Mid/late game it makes Apple Cider more efficient.
Pickaxe Saver II 50 Combined with Pickaxe Saver I, usage rate drops to 80%. Effectively grants 30% more mining duration—critical for Highland Hollows.
Deposit Detector II 100 Significantly increases white tile deposits containing pickaxes for deeper zones. Highly recommended for Highland Hollows or deeper.
Expanded Pig Pen I-V 75...175 Provides massive silver income via Truffles. Requires strong feed production and potentially 4x99 for the Pig Collar quest.
Send-o-Matic II 30 Essential for processing pigs into Bacon for quests or bulk silver income.
