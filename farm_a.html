Perk: (Category) Cost Notes
Orchard Land II & III: (Orchard) 125, 250 See Orchard Land I above. These are good to buy as your inventory size outgrows your orchard size.
Grape Juice Fountain & Waterfall: (Farming) 200, 300 Grape Juice Fountain and Waterfall are quite pricey, so when to purchase these depends a lot on your play style and progression speed. If you find yourself waiting for many long growing crops, these perks are good to pick up.
Extra Wishes: (Wishing Well) 125 See Extra Wish above. Extra Wishes is a little pricey so pick up as you need more wishing well throws.
Exploring Almanac: (Exploring) 30 Exploring is the slowest of the first 4 skills to level up and is often the last 99 people are stuck on. This perk is great for leveling up exploring faster to get to 4x99, useless after 99 exploring.
Sprint Shoes I: (Exploring) 100 Early game, Sprint Shoes 1 & 2 allow you to explore with less tapping due to exploring effectiveness, and mid and late game makes apple cider more efficient, so they are nice QoL and efficiency perks, but they are a little pricey. Once you find yourself tiring of manually exploring, or are using a lot of apple cider, these perks really shine! See Exploring Effectiveness for more information.
Pickaxe Saver II: (Mining) 50 Pickaxes are most often the limiting factor to mining in later zones. Combining Pickaxe Saver II with the Pickaxe Saver I skill perk will drop pickaxe usage rate to 80%, effectively giving you 30% more pickaxes which is extremely beneficial to mining deeper on zones past Highland Hollows.
Deposit Detector II: (Mining) 100 While expensive, Deposit Detector II when paired with the skill perk Deposit Detector I significantly increases the amount of single item white tile deposits you find while mining. Inside these deposits can be pickaxes for the next zone down, which are extremely helpful and where many if not the majority of your pickaxes for deeper zones come from. Highly recommend this if you're exploring at Highland Hollows or deeper.
Expanded Pig Pen I-V: (Livestock) 75, 100, 125, 150, 175 With the addition of Truffles to the game, Expanded Pig Pen perks provide a ton of additional silver. Highly recommended to pick them up when you're able to keep up with feed production and can buy all the pigs. The Pig Collar needs 4x99 to unlock the quest, but you can level up your pigs before then to take better advantage of truffles once you get there.
Send-o-Matic II: (Livestock) 30 Slaughtering pigs for bacon is needed for many quests or if you plan to sell bacon for extra silver income when slaughtering in bulk. See Slaughterhouse for more information.
